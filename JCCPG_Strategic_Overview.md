# 1. Company Identity

## Who We Are

JCC Property Group (JCCPG) is a property and homeowners' association
(HOA) management firm with a strong operational focus in North Carolina.
The company combines hands-on property management execution with
structured systems, documentation, and governance alignment.

JCCPG operates with a process-driven mindset, emphasizing:

-   Compliance with governing documents
-   Alignment with North Carolina statutes
-   Clear communication
-   Operational consistency
-   Scalable systems and documentation
